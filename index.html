About KP Mango

From our family farm in Talala Gir

KP Mango is a family-run mango farm located in Talala Gir, Gujarat. We grow Gir Kesar mangoes the traditional way — chemical-free, naturally ripened on the tree, and hand-picked at peak flavor. Our goal is to bring premium, honest fruit directly from the farmer to your home.

  • Small-batch, carefully sorted fruit
  • Traceable harvest from our farm to your doorstep
  • Ideal for families and premium buyers who value taste and safety
